<h2 class="Basics-headline">Find the Right Option for You</h2>
<p>Good dental care is important to your overall health. You know that. But do you know Medicare doesn't cover dental services? That means dental bills for things like routine cleanings, fillings, crowns, bridges, dentures, cosmetic services and root canals have the potential to take a bite out of your savings.<br/><br/>We offer both dental insurance plans and dental savings plan options to meet all your dental needs.</p>

</div>

<div class="OurSolutionsContent-headlineWrapper">
<div>
<h3 class="OurSolutionsContent-headline Headline--teal">Insurance Plan</h3>
</div>
</div>
<p>Our insurance is designed to help cover dental care that is essential to good health. Like your health insurance policy, dental insurance comes with an annual deductible, and  pays 100% for preventative services like two cleanings per year.<br/><br/>Mutual of Omaha provides coverage for basic services including fillings, and simple extractions, then it provides coverage for major services after a 12 month waiting period. We have two insurance solutions so you can find the best fit for your budget.</p>

<div>
<h3 class="OurSolutionsContent-headline Headline--blue">Savings Plan</h3>
</div>
</div>
<p>The Mutual of Omaha Dental Savings Plan gives you a reason to smile by granting you valuable access to a network of dentists and allowing you to manage your own dental expenses. Enjoy 5% to 60% savings on the cost of the dental care you need, plus deep savings on routine and even cosmetic dental care, including exams, unlimited cleanings, teeth whitening and veneers.<br/><br/>You can use the plan as often as you like for as little as $8.95 per month or $99 per year. To receive your savings, just select a participating dentist in your area and present your savings card during your visit. It's that simple to use this plan and save!</p><p>This is not insurance and is not intended to replace insurance.</p>
